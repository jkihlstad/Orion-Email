Add these keys to Info.plist for EventKit access:

NSCalendarsUsageDescription = "Orion needs access to your calendar to provide AI-powered scheduling and help you manage your time effectively."

NSCalendarsFullAccessUsageDescription = "Orion needs full access to your calendar to read events and suggest optimal meeting times."

NSCalendarsWriteOnlyAccessUsageDescription = "Orion needs write access to add AI-scheduled events to your calendar."

For Google OAuth, add URL scheme to Info.plist:
<key>CFBundleURLTypes</key>
<array>
    <dict>
        <key>CFBundleURLSchemes</key>
        <array>
            <string>orion-calendar</string>
        </array>
        <key>CFBundleURLName</key>
        <string>com.orion.calendar.oauth</string>
    </dict>
</array>
